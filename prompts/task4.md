### Prompt
Write a professional and highly polite letter providing constructive negative feedback to a software developer regarding their recent performance.

The letter should:
- Clearly identify specific mistakes or areas for improvement
- Explain the impact of these issues on the project, team, or outcomes
- Provide reasoning behind the concerns in a fair and objective manner
- Maintain a respectful, supportive, and constructive tone throughout
- Offer encouragement and, if appropriate, suggestions for improvement

After writing the initial version, review the letter and revise it if necessary to further improve politeness, clarity, and professionalism. Repeat this refinement process until the tone is consistently polite, constructive, and clear.

Output only the final improved version of the letter.
